Day 1 Day 2 Day 3

Green Week Activities

Day 1

Monday - anti drug presentation

It was a rainy day, however that did not stop us from going to the Romanian-American University in Bucharest. We listened to a few experts talk us through all the downsides of doing drugs and the struggles you ecounter when you try quitting. Most importantly we got to hear the stories of former drug users and the experiences they faced. These situations really resonated with us and made us realize just how vulnerable we are to drug usage.

Day 2

Tuesday - book swap activity

On the second day we decided to organize a book swap with the help of our english teachers. We thought it would be a great idea for students to trade their favourites books in order to save some money and also to help our planet. We also organized a “book lottery”, where the winner would get a special book. In the end we realised just how many of us read on a daily basis and got to speak about our favourite books.

Day 3

Wednesday - botanical garden visit

We started off the day by meeting at our highschool, where we discussed about ecology and what we can do to help our planet. Then we went to the botanical garden, where a guide was waiting to explain all the different plants inside the premise. Not only did we get to see a multitude of exotical plants and spend the day in nature, but we also got the chance to take a course in gardening.
